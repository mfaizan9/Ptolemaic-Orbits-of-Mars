Ptolemaic model diagram

Diagram of Ptolemy's Earth-centered model for the orbit of Mars. Earth is a blue dot at the center. Mars, the red dot, travels around a small circle called the epicycle; the center of that epicycle travels around a larger circle called the deferent. Combined, these motions trace the looping red path that reproduces the apparent retrograde motion of Mars in the sky. A yellow dot on the gray circle marks the mean Sun, which shares the epicycle's turning rate.
